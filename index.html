About Millionaire's Roast

A Springfield-based coffee brand built through the Cass County CEO program and grown through markets, direct orders, and hands-on roasting, with fresh single-origin coffee at the center of it.

Meet Maximiliano

Millionaire's Roast started through the Cass County CEO program, where Maximiliano Gomez turned a business idea into a real small-batch coffee brand rooted in his community.

Each batch begins with carefully sourced green coffee selected from documented single-origin, specialty-grade lots that cup 80+. The goal is not to make coffee feel exclusive. It is to offer genuinely high-quality coffee at a price normal people can come back to.

After graduating high school, Maximiliano moved to Springfield to pursue higher education in business. He continues Millionaire's Roast as part passion, part hobby, and part growing small business.

Now based in Springfield and still serving Beardstown and the greater Central Illinois area, the brand is growing through direct sales, Springfield markets, online ordering, and a new Beardstown partnership with The Cottage as the local home for our full lineup there.

Quick answers

Short and useful, so ordering stays easy.

Use our online shop for current coffees and Illinois shipping. In Beardstown, The Cottage at 201 W Main St is our main local point of contact and carries the full Millionaire's Roast lineup: bags, K-Cups, and bottled cold brew in both black and creamer variants.

Millionaire's Roast is based in Springfield, Illinois. We serve Springfield through local markets and online ordering, and Beardstown through The Cottage at 201 W Main St, an unmanned local farm stand connected with Rosebud & Co.

We currently offer five fresh single-origin coffees: Ugandan Rwenzori Kasese as our light roast, Costa Rican Jaguar Honey as our 2026 flagship medium roast, Peruvian Chonta G1 Washed, Guatemalan "La Morena", and Colombian Sugarcane Decaf. Our $18 Discovery Box includes three 3.5 oz samples: Ugandan light, Costa Rican medium, and Guatemalan dark, available whole bean or ground. We also offer 16 oz bottled cold brew for $5, brewed with Guatemalan "La Morena". Every coffee in the lineup is specialty grade, selected from coffees that cup 80+.

We source green coffee in bulk, but harvest timing differs by region and crop cycle. Once a coffee sells through, there is a high chance that specific lot is gone for now, not forever, and the menu keeps rotating based on demand and availability.

Specialty-grade coffee starts with better green coffee before it ever reaches the roaster. Our coffees are selected from documented 80+ lots, meaning they have been evaluated for cup quality rather than treated like ordinary commodity coffee. In the coffee industry, coffees are assessed through cupping: a standardized tasting process that looks at things like aroma, flavor, aftertaste, acidity, body, balance, sweetness, cleanliness, and defects. A score of 80 or higher is the traditional threshold for specialty-grade coffee. For customers, the result is simple: cleaner flavor, clearer tasting notes, and a more intentional cup.
